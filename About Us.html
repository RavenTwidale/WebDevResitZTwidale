Our Goal

Our goal is to provide a safe and engaging environment for home educated children across the nation. With access to a wide variety of subject areas from Maths and English to Ancient Greek Mythology and Willow Weaving workshops, there will be something for everyone.

As well as providing workshops for the children, we also aim to provide parents who are new to home education with free, impartial advice for anything relating to home education. This can be anything from help with setting up timetables, organising exams as an external candidate to help dealing with local authorities and officialdom.
